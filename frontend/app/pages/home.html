résumé  

Hire me! COVID-19 hit my last company hard, but before the layoffs I went from onboarding to delivering high-complexity features in just 2 months. If you’re looking for a skilled engineer to hit the ground running on your team, reach out!

Until recently, I was a full-stack web developer at Toast building React apps backed by Java microservices and DynamoDB.

Previously, I spent 4 years at Composable Analytics leading a team designing, bootstrapping, and implementing their one-of-a-kind enterprise data orchestration platform on .NET, SQL Server, and AngularJS.

In 2016, I graduated cum laude from Princeton University with a B.S.E. in Electrical Engineering and a certificate in Computer Science.

My senior thesis was a scalable network of temperature sensors for monitoring data center climates.

Way back, I interned at Vecna Technologies and Comcast.
